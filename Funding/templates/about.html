About the HealthNet System

The purpose of the HealthNet system is to recommends job vacancies to qualified professionals using Support Vector Machines and gradient boosting, optimizing the staffing process to improve patient care outcomes.

HealthNet System

HealthNet System is a dedicated website developed in 2024 that integrates advanced matching algorithms to recommend job vacancies to qualified healthcare professionals with relevant job vacancies, streamlining the staffing process and significantly enhancing patient care outcomes.

What the HealthNet System do ?

  • Healthcare professionals create profiles with their qualifications and preferences.
  • The system collects user information and job listings from healthcare facilities.
  • Algorithms like SVM and gradient boosting analyze profiles and job requirements for compatibility.
  • Professionals receive notifications about relevant job matches and application resources.
  • Employers quickly access qualified candidates, enhancing staffing efficiency.
  • Users provide feedback to refine algorithms and improve the platform experience.
